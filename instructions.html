Assessment Protocol

Follow these instructions carefully to complete your finger tapping assessment accurately.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Position yourself comfortably with adequate lighting. Ensure your hand is clearly visible in the camera view.
  2. You will complete 6 trials total - each hand will be tested 3 times. Follow the on-screen prompts for which hand to use.
  3. When each trial begins, tap your thumb and index finger together as quickly and accurately as possible for 20 seconds.
  4. Start with your fingers wide open, then make clear, deliberate tapping movements. Maintain consistent amplitude throughout each trial.
  5. Follow the visual guides on screen to position your hand correctly. The system will provide real-time feedback during the assessment.

Ready to begin? Make sure you've watched the demonstration video and understand the instructions.
